About SinBike

A bicycle-sharing system, public bicycle system, or bike-share scheme, is a service in which bicycles are made available for shared use to individuals on a very short term basis. Bicycle sharing began in Europe and now is available in more than 50 countries in the world. In this project, you will bring a new competitor, SinBike, in to Singaporean market.
