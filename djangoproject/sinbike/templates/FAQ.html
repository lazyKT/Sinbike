Getting Started

HOW TO USE SINBIKE SERVICES?

Step 1: Download the SinBike app on Google Play.
Step 2: Sign up an account with us.
Step 3: Top up your SinBike credits (minimum top-up S$10.00).
Step 4: Search for the nearest SinBike device on the Sinbike app.
Step 5: Scan the QR code on the device to unlock it.
Step 6: Enjoy your ride & ride safely.
Step 7: Locate your nearest designated parking area (’P’ icon) on the SinBike app.
Step 8: End your trip by parking at the designated parking area and manually locking the device.
Step 9: Verify your parking by scanning the QR code found at the designated parking area.

HOW MUCH DOES SINBIKE CHARGE?

Our rates are S$1.00 per 30 minute block.
You will be charged an additional S$1.00 once your ride duration exceeds each 30 minute block.
• 14 minute ride duration = S$1.00
• 34 minute ride duration = S$2.00
• 100 minute ride duration = S$4.00

I AM A FREQUENT USER, IS THERE ANY WAY I CAN SAVE MONEY?

If you are a frequent user of SinBike and wish to get the most bang for your buck, you can consider purchasing our SinBike Pass.
The SinBike Pass entitles you unlimited free trips per day with each trip not exceeding 30 minutes
(A charge of S$0.50/30min applies upon exceeding 30 minutes).
• 7-Day Pass: S$3.90
• 30-Day Pass: S$9.90
• 90-Day Pass: S$23.00

I PURCHASED A SINBIKE PASS, WHY AM I STILL BEING CHARGED AFTER MY TRIP?

SinBike Pass users are entitled to unlimited trips per day, however each trip is limited to a maximum
duration of 30 minutes. A charge of S$0.50/30min will apply should the user exceed the 30-minute trip durition

WHY IS THE FREE TRIP DURATION FOR THE SINBIKE PASS ONLY 30 MINUTES?

As we are a company that prides ourselves as a first mile & last mile transport service
provider, we strongly believe that 30 minutes is adequate time for you to complete your journey.

Trip

WHAT IS INDISCRIMINATE PARKING?

When a bicycle is not parked in a designated parking area, it will be classified as a case of indiscriminate parking.
Do note that designated parking areas appear as a ‘P’ icon on our app. Do remember to scan the QR code
found at the designated parking area to avoid getting any parking penalties. The parking QR code
can usually be found fixated on the ground or on a nearby wall.

WHY WAS I CHARGED A PENALTY OF S$5.00 FOR INDISCRIMINATE PARKING?

Users are required to end their trip by parking at designated parking areas and verifying their parking by scanning the QR code
found at the designated parking area. The penalty is imposed to deter users from parking indiscriminately,
reducing the number of stray bicycles left along the streets. The S$5.00 penalty is also used to account for
the costs of relocation and redistribution of our bicycles.

CAN I PAUSE MY TRIPS IN BETWEEN DESTINATIONS?

No, we do not have a pause function. In the instance that you need to stop your trip abruptly,
kindly park your bicycle properly at a designated parking area and verify your parking by scanning
the parking QR code found at the designated parking area.

HOW DO I SEND AN APPEAL REGARDING A PARKING PENALTY I RECEIVED?

If you received a S$5.00 parking penalty due to certain circumstances, you can send us an appeal via our in-app function under the ‘Help Centre’ tab.
Kindly select the appropriate category and attach any supporting documents (screenshots of any errors encountered or photos regarding any issues
encountered while parking), along with photos of the device being parked properly in a designated parking area.
Appeals would normally take approximately 3 working days (may vary due to high volume of feedback) to process.

WHY WAS MY APPEAL REJECTED?

All appeal cases are investigated by our support team with due diligence. Appeals are rejected when the appeal case is deemed to
have insufficient supporting evidence or if the evidence provided does not support the claims of the appeal.

WHAT SHOULD I DO IF I HAD AN EMERGENCY OR MET WITH AN ACCIDENT?

In the event of an emergency, the user is required to take relevant photos of said accident and submit them along with their appeal.
